✦ AI-powered

Built-in AI, not a bolt-on

Trakolo uses AI to cut manual triage work and catch problems before they become tickets — trained on your own workspace data, never shared across customers.

AI ticket triage & routing

Automatically categorizes, prioritizes, and routes incoming tickets to the right team — no manual sorting.

AI virtual agent

Resolves common requests (password resets, access requests, FAQs) instantly, before a human ever sees them.

Predictive asset alerts

Flags licenses likely to be under- or over-utilized, and predicts renewal risk before it becomes a cost surprise.

Smart resolution suggestions

Surfaces similar past tickets and their fixes as an agent types, shortening time-to-resolution.

Sentiment & escalation detection

Flags frustrated or high-risk conversations early so they can be escalated before they churn.

Natural-language reporting

Ask "which team missed the most SLAs last month?" and get a chart back — no dashboard building required.
