Pricing FAQ

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes apply on your next billing cycle.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes, annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly on Team and Business plans.

What happens after the free trial?

You can downgrade to the Free plan at any time — your data stays intact, no forced deletion.
