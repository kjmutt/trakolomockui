Root cause investigation
Firmware 4.2.1 on the 3rd-floor access points drops 802.11r fast-transition handshakes, killing VPN tunnels on roaming laptops. Confirmed by reproducing on a test device. Vendor ticket open; 4.2.2 beta fixes it in the lab.

Disable fast transition on the affected SSID — applied Jul 18. New occurrences dropped from ~6/day to 0. Permanent fix is tracked as development ticket DEV-1067 (AP firmware 4.2.2 profile), ready for rollout via change request CR-0093.
