Fix VPN drops when moving between floors

KB-0041 · Network & VPN · updated Jul 18 from PRB-0007
  1. This affects laptops roaming between 3rd-floor access points after the recent firmware update.
  2. A network-side workaround is already in place — most users need no action since Jul 18.
  3. If your VPN still drops: disconnect, turn wifi off and on, and reconnect to the corp-secure network.
  4. Still dropping? Log a ticket and mention PRB-0007 — it will be linked to the open problem automatically.
